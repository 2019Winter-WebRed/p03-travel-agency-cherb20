Bienvenue á Tahiti

Located in the Pacific Ocean, The Islands of Tahiti are a mythical destination. Tahiti is the largest island in French Polynesia. Tahiti's black-sand beaches, lagoons, waterfalls and 2 extinct volcanoes, make it a popular vacation destination.






















Things to do:

  • Visit the Robert Wan Pearl Museum in Papeete, Tahiti. It is the world's only Museum dedicated to Pearls.
  • Visit Marché de Papeete, a famous market located in Papeete, Tahiti.
  • Visit the Notre Came Cathedral in Papeete. It is a late 19th Century church that serves as the cathedral of the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Papeete.
  • Visit the outstanding waterfalls Tahiti has to offer. A few of their most popular waterfalls include Faarumai Waterfall, Cascade Vaimahuta Waterfall, and the Twin Waterfalls.
  • Participate in water sports such as wakeboarding, water skiing, wakeskating, and wakesurfing.
